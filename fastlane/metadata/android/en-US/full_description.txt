Open-source AI chat app that builds interactive screens, not just text. MorsVitaEst is a free ChatGPT alternative with a soul, a heartbeat, persistent memories, and reinforced learning — an AI assistant that evolves with every conversation you have.

Unlike ordinary chatbots, MorsVitaEst generates native interactive UI — buttons, forms, tables, charts, and full-screen apps — right inside the chat. Ask it to build a calculator, a quiz, a dashboard, or a to-do list, and it renders a real working interface you can tap, swipe, and interact with. This is AI beyond text.

🧠 Soul & Memory — A Chatbot That Knows You
Give MorsVitaEst a personality by writing its "soul." It remembers what matters across conversations — your preferences, past learnings, and known issues — and reinforces that knowledge over time. Memories that prove useful get promoted into its permanent personality. The more you chat, the smarter it gets.

💓 Heartbeat — Your Always-On Personal Agent
MorsVitaEst doesn't sleep. Its heartbeat system checks in on a schedule — reviewing pending tasks, emails, and trends — even when you're not chatting. Set the interval, active hours, and model. When something needs your attention, MorsVitaEst surfaces it. When everything's fine, it stays quiet.

⚡ 24 Services — One App, Every Model
Connect to ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, DeepSeek, Llama, Mistral, Grok, and 17 more providers. Run local models via Ollama or LM Studio for private, offline use. Add multiple accounts, reorder priorities, and let the smart fallback chain handle outages automatically. A built-in free tier means you can start chatting with zero setup — no API key needed.
